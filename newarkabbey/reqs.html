Novice Requirements

A candidate for Newark Abbey must be between the ages of 21- 35 years of age and must be psychologically, emotionally, physically, and spiritually healthy to live the monastic and community life.

A potential candidate for Newark Abbey first makes a few day visits to the Abbey. The vocation director after consultation with the Abbot may ask for the candidate to come for a longer visit to the monastery. If the candidate wishes to enter the monastery he may apply for admission to Postulancy.

To arrange a visit to the monastery, please contact the vocation director.

Postulancy Application Requirements

  • Recent physical exam records
  • Baptismal and confirmation records
  • Official transcripts from schools attended, including high school, university, graduate, and/or postgraduate school
  • Five (5) letters of recommendation (Pastor, Spiritual Director, Employer, Priest or Religious, and a relative)

To arrange a visit to the monastery, please contact the vocation director.
