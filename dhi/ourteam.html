Meet Our Team

Dr. Jeyasri Gunarajasingam BDS, DMD

Dr. Jeyasri Gunarajasingam received her undergraduate degree from the prestigious Peradeniya university in Candy, Sri Lanka. After emigrating to the United States in in 1983, she attended The Henry Goldman school of Dentistry at Boston University. It was her entrepreneurial spirit that motivated her to start the Dental Health International practice with her husband, Ponnadurai Gunarajasingam, in January of 1991.

Over two decades later, she continues to nurture and strengthen her first practice while also expanding her enterprise with Chelsea Dental Care and Lynn Dental Health. Jeyasri brings over twenty years of business experience to Dental Health International. Yet even with her extensive background in the dental field, she is committed to educating herself on new dental technologies and practices while also ensuring that her clients are happy and satisfied with their treatment.

In her spare time, Jeyasri enjoys watching the Food Network, taking French and Italian cooking classes, and walking her beloved Giant Schnauzer, Eli.

Dr. Oleg Agranovich DMD

Dr. Oleg Agranovich has been with Dental Health International for thirteen years as a general dentist. He attended the highly regarded Dovetsk State Dental University in the Ukraine. Upon graduating from Tufts in 2000, he began his career at Dental Health International, as he was instantly attracted to the family environment, challenging cases, and professionalism that our practice has to offer.

Dr. Parul Taneja BDS, DMD, MS

Dr. Parul Taneja received her DMD from Boston University Henry M. Goldman School of Dental Medicine in 2005. Her graduate degree in Orthodontics is from the University of Oklahoma Health Sciences Center. Dr. Taneja is a protege of Dr. Nanda, a legendary figure in the field of orthodontics. She also completed Master of Science degree in Oral Biology at the University of Oklahoma in 2002.

She is in full time practice in Waltham, Chelsea and Lynn. Dr. Taneja is a Clincial Assistant Professor at the Tufts University School of Dental Sciences. She is the director of the Biomechanics course for the graduate program. Dr. Taneja is also a guest lecturer at the pre-doctoral orthodontic program at the Boston University Goldman School of Dental Medicine.

Dr. Taneja has authored several articles in peer review journals. She has written the chapter on inter-disciplinary care in the textbook "Orthodontics - Diagnosis and Management of Malocclusion and Dentofacial Deformities" published by Elsevier. She is a recipient of the Massachusetts Dental Society (MDS) "Ten under Ten Award" in 2010. The award honors dentists who within the first ten years of practice have made significant contributions in the field of dentistry.

She is an active member of the American Association of Orthodontists, the Massachusetts Dental Society, Massachusetts Orthodontics Association and the Indian Dental Society.

For leisure Dr. Taneja, enjoys to reading, writing, dancing, music and traveling. She is yoga practitioner and is also a certified yoga instructor. She lives with her family in Boston.

Betty Perrello

Betty Perrello is our senior supervisor of dental assistants. She graduated from the Universidad Autonoma de Santo Domingo in the Dominican Republic. She also attended school at the Bryman Institute here in Boston, Ma, and has been with us for twelve years. Being a certified dental assistant allows her to effectively manage our team of dental assistants.

Betty is known as our clinical coordinator, as she arranges the schedule for the assistants. She also plays a vital role in ensuring that our sterilization systems are fully functioning. Her constant concern is that patients are receiving the safe and quality care that they deserve.

Betty spends most of her her free time with her daughter, and she enjoys going to the park to observe nature and relax.
